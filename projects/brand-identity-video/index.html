Brand Identity Video

CUPRA Catalunya Motor lacked a clear way to communicate who they were, where they came from, and what their connection to the CUPRA brand meant. They needed a video piece that could tell that story directly and with confidence across social media.

I led the full video editing and designed all the text visuals and motion graphics from scratch. I used official CUPRA brand videos as a visual reference to make sure the piece felt consistent with the brand's identity while still giving the dealership its own voice.

The final video gave the dealership a compelling and professional way to introduce themselves to their audience online, strengthening their brand presence on social media and putting a face to the leadership behind the business.
