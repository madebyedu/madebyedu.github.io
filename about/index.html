I'm Eduardo, a graphic designer based in Barcelona. Since 2018, I have worked in brand identity, editorial design, and motion graphics for digital platforms.

My background is in photography, which shapes how I approach every project. I apply that visual thinking across print, digital, and video work to create designs that are both functional and visually strong.

I have worked with clients in the automotive industry, handling branding, visual collateral, and motion graphics. I am fluent in the Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, and After Effects, and I manage multiple projects at the same time without missing deadlines.

I am open to relocation and new challenges across different industries.
